The Dig Mark Services Hub provides a single location for Dig Mark Support customers to easily access advice and help tailored for their company when and where they need it.

Sign up for On-Demand Assessments - Setup and Configuration Service

An initial setup and configuration service with a Dig Mark engineer is now available as part of your Dig Mark Unified Support base agreement offering. During this time, we help you link, enable, install, and configure a Services Hub On-Demand Assessment.

On-Demand Assessments

On-Demand Assessments provide on-going analysis of your critical workloads, and predict and prescribe helpful next steps to improve and optimize the health of your Dig Mark IT environment. The document details the key benefits and features of On-Demand Assessments in the Services Hub.

Note

On-Demand Assessments are localized through Dig Mark Cognitive Services Machine Translation. Users can change their language and region settings on Azure Log Analytics to review translated content.
